Personal Profile

Frenchie living the American Dream...

I am the last of three boys from Lyon France where I lived for the first 10 years of my life. I grew up learning about hard work and ethics through sports and the never ending mind games my father and brothers encouraged me to play. We moved to America early 90s where I stayed thru college.

After graduating from college, I decided to travel around the world for nearly 1 year which was an amazing experience. I spent a lot of time in Europe and bit in Asia as well. I enjoy getting immersed in different cultures, observing individuals, getting to know them, and building relationships. Then my wife had the opportunity to work in New York within fashion so we did it. It was an incredible experience and this amazing city taught us many professional traits that simply could not be learned anywhere else...

Our next journey took us to Wisconsin where I joined the start up Red Dot Square Solutions as the 3rd U.S. employee. We provided Virtual Reality capabilities to all major global CPG and retailers of the world. We quickly grew to over 60 employees before being acquired by WPP in 2009 at which time I was based in Minneaoplis.

I joined Target Corp. in 2012 where I have spent the best years of my career thus far. I am surrounded by an amazing team, the work is challenging, and I continue to learn.
